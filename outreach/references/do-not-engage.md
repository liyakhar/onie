# Do not engage

Skip discovery candidates that match these patterns. When in doubt, skip.

## Topics

- Giveaways, contests, airdrops
- Waitlist / "coming soon" with no workflow content
- Pure product launches with no shareable setup
- Politics, culture war, unrelated drama
- Crypto / NFT unless it's a genuine agent workflow post

## Post types

- Engagement bait ("like if you agree")
- Retweet farms
- Already a reply thread where you'd be the 50th "great post!"
- Competitor promo for another workflow directory (don't dogpile)

## Accounts

- Bots and repost aggregators
- Blocked or muted accounts (maintain a local list in `signals.yaml` → `blocklist`)

## When they already said no

- If someone declined a similar invite before, do not contact again
